## Overview

This document presents the complete 40-item KANO questionnaire administered to N = 282 valid respondents. Each item pair consists of a **functional** question ("If this feature IS present, how do you feel?") and a **dysfunctional** question ("If this feature IS NOT present, how do you feel?"). Response options follow the standard 5-point KANO scale.

## Notes

- Each item was presented in randomised order within its dimension block; dimension blocks were presented in fixed order (B1→B7).
- The functional and dysfunctional sub-questions for the same item were separated by a minimum of 8 intervening items to reduce carryover bias.
