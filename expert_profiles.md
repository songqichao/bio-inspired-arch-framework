## Recruitment

Experts were identified through professional networks and peer-reviewed publication records. All five confirmed familiarity with both biomimetic design principles and multi-criteria decision-making prior to participation. Each expert independently completed the pairwise comparison matrices; no discussion between experts occurred during the scoring phase. Individual matrices were aggregated post-hoc using the geometric mean method.
